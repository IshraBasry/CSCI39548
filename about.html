About ITHAA

Surrounded by marine life beneath the crystal-clear waters of the Maldives, ITHAA offers a once in a lifetime underwater dining experience. ITHAA combines the freshest seafood with rich tropical flavors, served in an elegant and serene atmosphere. Schools of reef fish drift past curved glass walls as flavors unfold on your palate. Join us and make memories beneath the sea.
